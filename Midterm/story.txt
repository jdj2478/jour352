In St. Landry Parish, La., three black churches burned down from arson within
10 days in late March and early April. Before Monday, a GoFundMe for the churches
had raised just below $50,000 in rebuilding aid. After a Twitter campaign urged
people to remember the Louisiana churches amidst the devastation of Notre Dame,
the total skyrocketed to $1 million by Wednesday.
